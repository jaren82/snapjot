## Detailed description

Point at anything on a page and say "here!" — in one breath.

SnapJot is a deliberately small screenshot tool for work feedback:

⚡ ONE FLOW
Click the icon (or Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+S) → drag to select (or press Enter for full screen) → annotate → Copy. Paste straight into Slack, Notion, email with Cmd/Ctrl+V. No editor window, no extra steps.

🧰 JUST THE TOOLS YOU NEED
• Box — outline the area you mean
• Arrow sticker — a chunky inspection-style arrow that points at the exact spot
• Text label — a clean rounded note chip
• 5 preset colors + full RGB picker (with eyedropper)
• Undo, save as PNG

🔒 PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• No account. No sign-up.
• No cloud. Your screenshots are never uploaded anywhere — they go to your clipboard and nowhere else.
• Minimal permissions: SnapJot can only see the tab you explicitly invoke it on, at the moment you click.
• No analytics, no tracking, no data collection. (See our privacy policy.)

If you've been burned by screenshot tools that upload your captures to public URLs, this one is for you.

📝 HONEST NOTES
• Free version adds a small "created by SnapJot" credit at the bottom-right of captures.
• Captures the visible area of the page (full-page scrolling capture is not the goal — staying fast and simple is).

Made by an indie developer who uses it every day. Feedback welcome!
